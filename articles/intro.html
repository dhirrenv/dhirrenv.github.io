Why Investing Is Hard

28/01/2026 · 5 min read

Hi guys a few thoughts before i start actually talking about stocks:

im not gna just say stocks that are gonna go up so that u guys can copy the move and profit off that. I know everyone wants the easy money (so did I) but the truth is if u just follow other people and take the easy way out, u will eventually lose money. If it was easy, everyone would be doing it. the whole reason there is money to be made in the stock market is because it is hard to do. theres this saying "if you dont know who the idiot at the poker table is, you are the idiot" so if u dont know exactly why ur doing what ur doing and ur just following someone like a sheep, you are the idiot and they are using u as exit liquidity (will learn about that soon). I want u guys to learn about how these things work so that u can come up with ur own ideas and strategies, because those are the ones that will actually make u profitable.

u guys need to know that this is still extremely risky. there are ways to minimise risk that i will talk about but no matter what there is always a chance you could lose money. u should ALWAYS TRADE WHAT UR OK WITH LOSING. If u feel scared of taking a trade and u dont want to part with that money, DONT TAKE THE TRADE. U are either not confident enough or u are allocating too much money to one trade.

pls note that everything i say here is my opinion and my own experience ONLY so take everything with a grain of salt. I am only human and i do make mistakes very often, do not follow me blindly. Think for yourself and evaluate my ideas for yourself before you internalise them. I will probably be wrong about something soon, and thats just the nature of this field. just be prepared to lose some money and take a small amount of risk.

i will be using terminology that might be a bit complicated at first, but once u get used to it, it will feel like second nature. ill try my best to include explanations if i do use non-layman terms but its a lot easier to use trading terminology when explaining stuff.

finally, i wanna reiterate that investing is HARD. a lot of ppl (maybe even u guys) will get bored and decide that its not worth all the hassle, and thats perfectly fine. Its always better to stay away than do something reckless when ur not fully informed or aware. only do this if ur prepared to spend time learning and making mistakes on ur own. good luck guys!
