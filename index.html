About

I'm a student based in Singapore with a deep interest in financial markets, macroeconomics, and the structural forces that drive price. This site is where I develop and publish my own analysis — written independently, not to summarise what others say, but to work through what I actually think.

My work covers everything from first principles (how markets price anything at all) to real positions I hold and why. I believe the best way to learn markets is to have skin in the game and be honest about your reasoning.
